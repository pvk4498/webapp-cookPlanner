About Cook's Aid

Welcome to Cook's Aid, your personal assistant for managing catering ingredient lists! This web application is designed specifically for cooks who provide on-demand services for various functions like marriages, birthdays, devotional events, and more.

Our goal is to simplify the process of creating detailed ingredient lists. You can easily categorize ingredients, set their required quantities, and add custom items.

Once your list is ready, you can download it as a PDF or an image, or conveniently share it directly via WhatsApp with your clients or team members. We hope Cook's Aid makes your planning smoother and more efficient!
